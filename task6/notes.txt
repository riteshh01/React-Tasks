Phase 5 Task: "The Movie Encyclopedia" (Navigation & Details)
Abhi tak tumhari app sirf movies ki list dikha rahi thi. Ab hum ise ek poori website banayenge jisme Routing hogi.

Aapka Mission:

Home Page (/): Yahan wahi movie search bar aur list hogi jo tumne pehle banayi.

Detail Page (/movie/:id): Jab user kisi movie card par click kare, toh wo ek naye page par chala jaye jahan us movie ki poori detail ho (Plot, Actors, Ratings, Director).

Navbar: Ek simple navigation bar jo har page par dikhe (Home aur About links ke saath).

Isme tum kya naya seekhoge?
React Router DOM: Browser ki URL history ko manage karna.

BrowserRouter, Routes, Route: Routes setup karne ke liye.

Link Component: Bina page refresh kiye navigate karne ke liye (<a> tag ka upgrade).

useParams Hook: URL se ID nikalne ke liye (e.g., /movie/tt12345 se tt12345 lena).
